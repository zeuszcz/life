# Character sprite credits

The character spritesheets in this folder come from the **Universal LPC
Spritesheet** (Liberated Pixel Cup) collection.

- Source: https://github.com/jrconway3/Universal-LPC-spritesheet
  (online generator: https://sanderfrenken.github.io/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator/)
- Project: Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC) — https://lpc.opengameart.org/
- **License: dual-licensed CC-BY-SA 3.0 and GNU GPL 3.0.**

LPC art is the work of many contributors. Primary/base authors commonly credited
across the body, hair and clothing sheets include (non-exhaustive):

- Stephen Challener (Redshrike)
- Johannes Sjölund (wulax)
- Manuel Riecke (MrBeast)
- Joe White, Matthew Krohn (makrohn), Marcel van de Steeg (Bertram)
- Daniel Eddeland, Lanea Zimmerman (Sharm), and the wider LPC community

Full per-asset attribution is maintained in the source repository's `CREDITS`
files and on OpenGameArt. Because these assets are CC-BY-SA 3.0 / GPL 3.0, this
project (which redistributes them) is likewise shared under compatible terms for
the artwork.

Files here were extracted from the universal 64×64 sheet layout (13×21 frames;
walk-cycle rows 8–11 = up/left/down/right).
